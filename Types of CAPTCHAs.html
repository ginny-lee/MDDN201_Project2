Types of CAPTCHAs

There are many different types of CAPTCHA’s on the internet. They include word problems, math problems, picture identification probelms, socialmedia sign in method, timebased probelms, and reCAPTCHAs.

Word problem CAPTCHAs:
The word problem CAPTCHA’s are one of the most common CAPTCHA’s on the internet. For this users need to decycipher the word that is being displayed and write it in the area provided. This is a very effective and beneficial method of testing as there is an option to hear the word being presented for users that are visually impared.

Math Problem CAPTCHAs:
Maths problem CAPTCHA’s are much like the word problem CPATCHA’s. The only difference is that the math CAPTCHA’s are maths. Users are asked to answer a simple math problem ie. 5+2. This may seem too simple however, AI does not have the capability to solve these simple equations which makes this type of CAPTCHA very secure.

Picture Identification CAPTCHAs:
Picture identification CAPTCHA’s gives users a bunch of pictures (usually 9) relating to a specific theme ie animals. Users then will have to select all the pictures that are relate to the theme. This type of CAPTCHA usually only uses basic simple photos for humans however, still hard for AI.

Social Media login CAPTCHAs:
The social media login CPATHCA is basically explained in the name. For this CAPTCHA the users are asked to sign in to one of their social medai accounts verify themselves ie. Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat, Google. This form of CAPTCHA is secure as robots do not have a social media account to use. The negative side to this CAPTCHA is that the users personal information is being leaked. However, it is a secure method.

Timebased CAPTCHAs:
Timebased CAPTCHAs is an interesting one. This CAPTCHA keeps track of the time it takes the user to fill out their information on a form. Humans usually take a decent amount of time to fill the form whereas robots take very less time. This method of CAPTCHA is secure as it is very easy to tell if the user is a rotbot or a human. The downside of this is that users are sharing personal information like the social media login CAPTCHA method.

RECAPTCHAs:
RECAPTCHAs are a type of CAPTCHA that was created by google in 2014. It works a by detecting the behaviour when users are presented with a simple task. The most common task is to tick a box indicating “I am not a Robot”. When users peform this action the RECAPTCHA tracks the movement and determins if the user is a human or not. For the example the method they use is if the box is checked directly in the middle it is a Robot if not the user is human. This is also a very secure CAPTCHA method.

These are the top 6 CAPTCHAs that are on the internet. There are many other different CAPTCHAs that are not as common but are still used to protect the internet.
